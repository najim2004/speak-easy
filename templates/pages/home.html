Course Features

Combine live classes, practice tools, and AI-powered feedback. Designed to make learning Japanese simple and effective.

Live Classes

Our live classes are conducted by experienced Japanese language teachers who guide students step by step. In these sessions, you can interact directly with the instructor, ask questions, and participate in real-time discussions. The classes also focus on pronunciation, conversation practice, and real-life communication so that you can build confidence in speaking Japanese.

Practice Lab

The Practice Lab is an interactive learning space where you can strengthen your Japanese skills through different tools. It includes grammar sheets, quizzes, flashcards, listening exercises, and speaking practice activities. These tools allow you to review lessons, test your knowledge, and practice regularly so that you can improve faster and retain what you learn.

AI Homework

With AI-powered homework checking, you can submit your writing or assignments and receive instant feedback. The system analyzes your sentences, identifies grammar mistakes, and suggests improvements in vocabulary and sentence structure. This helps you quickly understand your errors and continuously improve your writing skills.

Progress Tracking

Our progress tracking system allows you to monitor your learning journey easily. You can see how many lessons you have completed, track your quiz scores, and observe improvements in different skills. This feature helps you stay motivated and understand where you need more practice.

Mentor Support

Whenever you face difficulties in your learning journey, mentors are available to guide you. They help answer your questions, clarify difficult topics, and provide advice on how to study more effectively. Mentor support ensures that you never feel stuck while learning Japanese.

Career Guide

Learning Japanese can open many opportunities for jobs and higher studies. Our career guidance feature helps you understand different career paths related to the Japanese language. You will receive information about certifications, job opportunities, and study options that can help you plan your future.
